Our Story

Achieve Educational Corporation was established in March 2000 as an after-school test preparation program. 24 years and over 50,000 students later, Achieve became a leader in education. With our valuable experience, we select a limited number of students and guides the student and their parents through the college process from beginning to end. As a consultant, we have only one goal:
